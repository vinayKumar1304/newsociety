School for Labour children

The organization has been running Two schools for child laboures in the town areas of Raebareli. The programme was sanctioned by Assistant Labour commissioners of district Raebareli with the objectives of bringing child labourers to Normal life and make them part of the main stream of the development. The organization has done a survey in the project area and found about 540 laboure children. Out of which a total of 100 children who really want care and support are enrolled in these schools. Recognizing the fact that these children do not have access to formal education and are a source of cheap labour due to poverty, SGSS has started to provide informal education, nutrition and health checkups and involvement in recreational activities.

Rural Hygiene & Sanitation Programme

The organization has been working for ensuring proper hygiene and Sanitation in rural areas for last several years. For this purpose it has trained Self Gelp Group volunteers for construction of low cost latrines and cleaning of ponds and drainages and keeping the environment clean and hygienic. These volunteer are actively participating in its hygiene and sanitation programme and creating awareness about the environment conservation and sanitation including water harvesting to the support of public. In order to make this effort replicate in another location also it has now formed more volunteers from the nearest location and providing training.

Environment Awareness Programme

Water is the most essential requirnment for the life after oxygen has become highly polluted due to increased industrialization used of drinking water for many bad things and various other factors which has contributed to the increase in the water pollution. In order to knowing the fact of above the organization has conducted Environment conservation activities:- Under this programme it has done plantation of trees, chlorination of open wells in the Raebareli district. People were given information on the role of panchayti Raj Institutions in promoting environmental sanitation. In these programme about 500 people including farmers participated. During the awareness programme people were told about Land Reclamation Measures and use of sustainable agriculture practices for the safeguard of environment.

Consumer Awareness Programme

It is generally found that the consumers, deposit of paying competitive prices are offered low quality products by the companies. Though the products are packed in a very attractive packets but their quality happens to be very low. This way the producers and sellers cheating the consumers every day. The situation is not too different in the case of services also. In order to check the above situation unlike the previous years this year to the organization has conducted a consumer awareness programme at different location of districts Raebareli with the aim of develop knowledge and awareness to consumers about Act and their legal roghts etc. During this programme officials from consumer welfare board and hundreads of persons from the area were participated. Rallies of about 300-200 persons was also organized covering 7 kms through local villages of Raebareli. These persons were carried placards prominently displaying messages of consumer protection rights.

Celeberation of National Festivals:

Nutan Education & Welfare Society has organized various programme to remind the days of pre-independence and to arouse the feeling of patriotism the organization has oraganized various programme on the occasions of independence day republic day during the financial year 2005-06 also. The Programme was a major success on this occasion national anthem was sung and the national flag was hosted with the full honour. During these functions cultural/ competition (Posters/Speach) programme comprising nukkad nattak, puppet shows on the post independence Indian history was arranged by the society. The students of various Primary School and members of the society participated in these programme. At the end of the programme prizes were distributed among the winners of the contest.

Annual General body meeting

As previous years this year too the organization has conducted an annual general body meeting under the chief ship of Mrs. Anita Singh, Secretary Organization. Where in physical & financial achievements of the year 2005-06 were discussed. Copies of audited statement of expenditure was distributed among the members and view and reaction of the participants were obtained and annual action plan for the financial year 2006-07 were presented.

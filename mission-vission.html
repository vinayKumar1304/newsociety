Vision, Mission of the organization

Vission-

Vision of the organization is to empower resource poor community in managing development and welfare activities/schemes and local resource to meet their need in a participatory manner and collaborative spirit.

Mission-

The mission of the organization to work for sustainable holistic development through empowering rural community by providing quality education, health, family welfare, vocational training and creating awareness Conservation of environment and inculcation of values and culture” thereby achieving the over all objectives of contributing to the all-round development of the deprived section of the society.

The organization has headquarters at its own building at Raebareli district of Uttar Pradesh. Head office and field offices are well equipped with physical and human resources, telephone, computer, furniture, printer and fixture etc.

Nutan Education & welfare society, Bastepur, Raebareli focuses efforts and supports to the integarated long-term development of the deprived section of the community. For achieving the above objectives the organization has adapted the following working strategies:-

Achievments

Achieving holistic development through people’s participation by:-

  • Creating a learning society;
  • Ensuring women empowerment;
  • Ensuring health facilities;
  • Stabilizing population; and
  • Creating sustainable economic growth and thereby bringing improvement in quality of life.

For strengthening the capacity of each and every person of community with particular emphasizes on women, other operational strategies adopted are:-

  • Convergence of all development efforts for holistic development;
  • Community participation at all stages of project planning, implementation, monitoring & evaluation and thereby focusing on sustainable interventions.

Networking

The organization has developed strong networking with other voluntary organization working in through out the country and has also established very good rapport with various National, International organizations, different government departments. It has got adequate technical and managerial strength to provide guidance and give technical assistance to these voluntary organizations.

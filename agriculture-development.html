Agriculture Development

Agriculture Development

The organization with the objective to promotion of agriculture productivity has implementation different agriculture development activities with the support of UPKAR, Lucknow and Department of Agriculture for last several years. During implantation of these activities in the field it has realized the there is an urgent need of improving the scientific/technical capacity of farmers. In order to achieve it the organization has established a farmer’s school with the objectives:

  • To create awareness among them about the food protection;
  • To acquaint them about the use of technically suitable agriculture instruments for reducing their constraints out of workload;
  • To disseminate information about latest technology in the field of agriculture development;
  • To make them able to resolve different problems including gender issue in the field of agriculture;
  • To increase ability of gender sensitization in the area of agriculture.

This school is now running successfully with the support of male & female farmers of the project area.

Training workshop on preparation of bio-pesticides.

Nutan Education & welfare Society, Bastepur, Raebareli with the support of trainers of its farmers’ school has organized a training workshop on preparation of bio-pesticides from the locally available resources and its importance and usage, instead of using costly chemical pesticide available in the market. Farmers are also informed about the ill effects of chemical pesticides. During the programme about 200 persons (farmers) are participated.

Fruit, Vegetable plantation programme:

A project with the support of green India Scheme of Ministry of Environment and Forest. National Afforestation & Ecodevelopment Board, Government of India was sanctioned this organization. Under this project the organization has established a Fruit, Vegetable Plant Nursery in Raebareli district. This nursery supports the organization for income generation and providing training to farmers on techniques improving agriculture productivity and maintenance of plant nursery. This nursery also provides farmers space for income generation and thereby improving their quality of life.

Farmers exposure visit programme:

The organization has conducted a exposure visits of 705 farmers from the district Raebareli to different Agriculture development institutes in Uttar Pradesh. Wherein these farmers were given knowledge and make them aware about new agriculture techniques such as preparation of pesticides, Vermi compost, technically suitable agriculture instruments to improve agriculture productivity through which strengthen their economic condition and improve quality of life. The organization has also trained farmers on Nadep Compost and Vermi Compost under National Environment awareness programme in the district Raebareli and Sidharth Nagar.

Future planning:

The organization will continue its programme in the field of agriculture development such as production of cheap and quality seeds of verity vegetables & crops, training of farmers on generating awareness on technically suitable agriculture instruments to improve agriculture productivity, preparation of cheap and better pesticides, ravin stabilization programme etc in the coming years.

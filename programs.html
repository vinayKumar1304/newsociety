Education:

The organization started contributing towards education for last several years. As the organization’s one of the idea is focused to implement the programme “Education for all”, to accomplish this, it is running a primary School at Raebareli district-remotely located from the district H.Q. and has a total number of 365 students, from poor and disadvantage sections of rural areas. Besides the students from the previous sessions a 42 new students were added during the current financial year. This school has 7 trained teachers and 13 other staff. This school has also providing space for organization different awareness camps, training and workshops etc. A glance at Education status and many other development works of the district is a testimony to the success of this school.

Literacy programme for illiterates

The programme is being organization literacy classes in rural areas of the block Ganpatgunj with the aim to make illiterates to literate. Beyond reading and writing’ It is poised to give a new sense of direction to the beneficiaries of this programme. Developmental literacy, legal literacy, environmental literacy, consumer literacy, health literacy, agriculture literacy, culture literacy, economic literacy, heritage literacy etc. and various levels of vocational training programme were also undertaken. A total number 800 person benefited under this programme.

Future Planning

In the coming year the organization will carry its efforts further in the field of education & promotion of literacy.

Vocational Training

  • Training of women & Farmers

Training on Handicraft for women & Mashroom cultivation for farmers

Under the head the organization with the support DIC Sultanpur and Department of Agriculture is being provided Vocational training on Handicraft, Tailoring, Mashroom cultivation and Vermi compost for women and farmers of district Sidharth Nagar. A total number 200 women form economically weaker section of the Handicraft training programme. Through training of Mashroom cultivation about 150 farmers are got space for their income generation and thereby improved their quality of life.

Future Planning

The organization is planning to carry out its effort of vocational training to the remote localities of its operational areas.

Women empowerment through SHGs and awareness generation.

The organization has conducted number of women empowerment activities in districts Raebareli of Uttar Pradesh. The beneficiaries have been told about their role in society, reproductive rights, right to property, right to income and role in self governance and reservation for women in different programs. In order to instill confidence in women and make them self reliant, the organization has formed 127 women self-help groups (WSHGs) of 4082 members at Raebareli with the support of NABARD, Group activities are not only making these women economically independence, but also made them conscious about their health, family welfare and legal rights to improve their quality of life. Besides this the organization has also formed Male-Female Mitra Mandal (M&F SHGs) with the support of NABARD under the scheme of vikas Volunteer Vahini. These volunteers groups providing nutrition and safety support to the SHGs. These Mandals are the examples and model of gender sensitization of the community where the organization is working.

Swarnajayanti Gram Swarajgar Yojana.

A project with objective to develop a holistic programme for poor persons for self employment through Self Help Group, Capacity building. Training, Selection of key activities, infrastructure build up, technology and marketing support etc. has been implementation by Nutan Education & Welfare Society, Bastepur, Raebareli. The project is being implemented with the support of department of Rural Development Authority, Raebareli. In order to achieve the above objectives the organization has formed 112 self help groups of 715 women and 512 men members in the above villages. The members of these groups are now selected in grade first and linked with various banks.70 percentage of these groups are exclusively for women.

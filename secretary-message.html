Secratory’s Message

From the Secratory’s desk

Nutan Education And Welfare Society, Bastepur, Raebareli began its operations in the year 1999 for the welfare of marginalized and underprivileged section of the community particularly women. The organization that for improving the lives of community, education & skill building activities can play a major role and can empower the so that thee can access the services meant for them. The journey of past 9 years have seen strategic shift from only education driven programme to the holistic development programmes. The organization is being implemented several programmes in the area Health & Family welfare, Education, Skill / Capacity building, Women empowerment, Impowerment of Agriculture and Natural Resources, social Welfare and most importantly development of community based groups.

The organization’s programmes have evolved based on community perception and needs and from the welfare-oriented mission to institution building and sustainable development. Community participation is now taken as the strategic base of our programmes. We gave grown to a team of 58 staff with 55% women, which is a testimony of our commitment to empower women in all walks of life.

I take this opportunity to thanks our donors and well wishers their continued support in terms of providing financial resources as well as providing guidance to our programmes.

I am pleased to put forward the Annual Progress Report of the organization for the year 2005-2006, which is possible due to the hard work and commitment of whole team of the organization. This annual report gives a reflection of our activities and results achieved.

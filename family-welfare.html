Health & family Welfare

Reproductive, Child, Health & Population stabilization activities.

Organization has been working for Health & Family welfare programme for the eight years. As in the previous years this year also organization has conducted several Reproductive & Child health & population stabilization related activities with objectivities of:

  • Antenatal care & facilitating safe deliveries
  • Children immunization in the age group of 0-5 years;
  • Prevention and management of RTI/STDs and HIV/AIDS
  • Promotion of small family norms

In order to achieve the above objective the organization has conducted number of IEC activities through folk media in the rural villages and conducted awareness camps/melas in different locations of districts Raebareli, Uttar Pradesh through mobilization of community members and provided health facilities such as antenatal checkups, TT immunization, immunization to children, distribution of contraceptives, counseling services etc.

This programme is being implementing with the support and coordination from the public health system and district health administration. The organization has shared information above the type of services that can be availed from the government health infrastructure, and created a working environment for the ANM to provide such services.

Nutan Education & Welfare Society, Bastepur, Raebareli, has been working for Mother and child care services in Raebareli district of the state. Through this project NEWS will endeavor to provide services to the target group in collaboration with the existing government health machinery and ensure that the services really reach to the target group.

Objectives of the project

The man objective of the project is to provide preventive and curative health care service to the underprivileged section of the community especially the poor women of the proposed areas.

For ensuring the health services to beneficiaries in the villages a health post is established at the project area. This center has the provision of a Full time registered Medical Doctor, One registered ANM/Nurse, A trained TBA and a Health Coordinator/ supervisor for motivation, referral and ensuring patient to the health post. This center is providing the services such as limited, curative health care for e.g. headache, fever and common cold. Diarrhea, RTI, skin element, warm infestation, anemia etc. to the disadvantage population in the project area and refer serious cases to higher centers. Besides this health post also providing health services to the identified pregnant women, Lactating mothers and children in the project area.

Children immunization programmes

The organization has worked for child health programme in differen locations of Raebareli districts. In these location women are organized in groups and they were motivated to bring children to the sub centre and PHC for getting immunization services. Importance of breast feeding was also heighted. Besides this the organization has also organized several pulse polio immunization booths. The organization has also conducted immunization camps to save children from six life taking diseases BCG, Triple vaccinations and Khasra immunizations were administered to the children between the age group of 0-1 years and booster doses were given to children of 2-5 years. Besides this Vitamin-A doses were also given to the children. Management of diarrhea and pneumonia booths in various locations with the support of district health administration and volunteers of the organization.

HIV/AIDS awareness & prevention programme

AIDS destroys families, first turning stable households into one-parent families, and then leaving parentless children at the mercy of grandparents or institutions. It attacks the powerless and most vulnerable women unable to protect them selves, youths who do not understand its risks and infants at the time of birth or while breast feeding. In addition to suffering the debilitating effects of the disease, many are rejected by family, friends and their community. Most of the peoples may choose to hide their disease from friends and family for fear of abandonment. In order to address the this issue and control HIV transmission and prevention of AIDS in the deadly disease the Nutan Education & Welfare Society with the support of district health administration has organized AIDS awareness programme through IEC/BCC & Advocacy.

Besides general community it has also covered road users including truckers, construction laborers and transport workers by generating awareness about its ill effects. In order to make this interesting number of magic shows, street plays, Puppet shows etc. are organized at different location of its project area. Several meeting with truckers, road users, migrant labourers and other high risk groups at dabbas, road side shops, passing through try section and other main crossings of the road and temporary boarders of labourers working on the highways construction/repair works etc. was done.

Plan of action

Now the organization plans to work in collaboration with NACO and other agencies who are supporting for implementation of prevention of this deadly disease and providing care and support for the PLWHAs of its programme area. It is planning to establish drop-in, counseling and testing centers and conducting more focused and innovative IEC and BCC programmes for creating awareness, condom promotion/adoption among the risk groups as well as general population.

Future planning for promotion of Health & Family welfare activities:

With the continued success in the field of health and family welfare the organization is planning to intensity the programme in the virgin and newer areas of the its projects districts. It aims to bring newer approaches in the sector and also to introduce sustainable models which can be replicated on a larger level.

For sustaining the health and family welfare project activities the organization is planning to converge it with continuing self Help Groups.
